Sourabh 65 Recipe

Ingrdiants

For the marination

  • Chicken - 500g
  • Chicken 65 Masala - 1TB
  • Garam Masala - 1TB
  • Chicken Masala - 1TB
  • Chilli Powder - 1TB
  • Salt - According to taste
  • lemon juice - 1TB
  • Ginger-Garlic paste - 1TB
  • Time - 10 mins

Instructions

  1. First take frying pan and add half ltr of sasive yenne
  2. wait for some time to yenne bisi
  3. After that sprinkel some water to yenne
  4. When yenni start chit put,chit put, chit put, then add marinted chicken to yenni [ only 4-6 pc per round]
  5. After 5 min take jari and up-down down-up up-down hollas
  6. Remove all fried chicken from karu balagi
  7. Eat and enjoy
